Privacy Policy

This app does not require account registration.

The app may collect limited data automatically for app functionality, diagnostics, and advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services such as Firebase and Google AdMob.

Firebase is used for basic app functionality and diagnostics (such as crash reports and performance data).

Google AdMob is used to display third-party advertisements within the app.

Advertising

Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as advertising identifiers) to serve and measure advertisements. This data is not linked to the user’s identity.

The app does not use data for tracking users across apps or websites owned by other companies
